Welcome to the workshop:

Shotgun metagenomics data analysis

Instructors: Sahar Bagheri & Juan Jovel

Date, 2025 (9:00 am - 4:00 pm) ❃ Room ❃ Registration Fee CAN $300.00 ♥ ♥ ♥

Unravel the hidden world beneath our feet, inside our bodies, and across our planet! Join our hands-on Shotgun Metagenomics Data Analysis Workshop and master the tools to decode complex microbial communities.

Why settle for fragments when you can capture the complete picture?

In two intensive days, you'll transform from data-overwhelmed to analysis-confident:

- Dive beyond 16S sequencing into the full genomic potential of microbial communities
- Master cutting-edge bioinformatic pipelines that turn raw sequencing data into actionable insights
- Discover hidden metabolic pathways and novel microbial functions that are reshaping our understanding of biology

Whether you're tracking antibiotic resistance, exploring the human microbiome, or searching for novel enzymes, this workshop equips you with the computational arsenal to extract meaning from metagenomic complexity.
