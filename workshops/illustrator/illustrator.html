Welcome to the workshop Scientific Figures with Adobe Illustrator

Transform your research visuals from basic to brilliant in our comprehensive workshop designed specifically for researchers.
Master the essential tools and techniques in Adobe Illustrator to create clear, professional figures that effectively communicate your findings.


What you'll learn

- Vector graphics fundamentals for creating infinitely scalable figures that look crisp at any resolution
- Best practices for preparing figures that meet journal requirements and enhance manuscript submissions
- Techniques for converting data plots and microscopy images into publication-quality graphics
- Time-saving workflows to streamline your figure preparation process

Hands-on training includes

- Creating publication-ready multi-panel figures
- Designing consistent, professional legends and labels
- Working with color schemes that remain clear in both digital and print formats

- Managing layers and groups for complex figure organization

Intended audience

Perfect for: Graduate students, postdocs, research staff, and faculty who want to elevate their scientific figures from acceptable to exceptional.
